Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 10, 2026

1. Overview

2248: Brain Merge Puzzle ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected (via AdMob):

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Device model and OS version
  • App usage data (crash reports, performance)

Information You Provide (Stored Locally Only):

  • Profile nickname and avatar choice
  • Game preferences (sound, theme, effects)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To save your game progress and preferences locally
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To improve app performance and fix crashes

4. Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We do not have servers, do not collect personal information, and do not share your data with third parties except as required for advertising via Google AdMob.

5. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob - Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID (resettable in device settings)
  • IP address and device information
  • Ad interaction data

Learn more about Google's privacy practices: https://policies.google.com/privacy

No other third-party services or analytics tools are used.

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally:

  • You can reset all data from Settings → Reset Game
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • You can reset your advertising ID in device settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All data is stored locally and cannot be accessed by us.
